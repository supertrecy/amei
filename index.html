Our Method

Micro blood-vessel analysis and breath analysis are the methods we invest for the development of early disease detection.

1. Micro Blood-Vessel Analysis

It has been developed in Germany and Switzerland and widely used for general diagnostics and preventive medicine. In addition to detecting eye problem, precise prediction about cardiovascular problems can be made 5-15 years ahead of actual breakout of the illness, giving ample time to implement corrective measures.

Early-generation equipment is already on the market, but it is still mainly used in ophthalmology; only a few research institutes have begun to use the equipment for general cardiovascular analysis and disease prevention.

Our aim is Introducing this technology to Chinese research centers (and eventually to the generally market) and to further improve and develop the hardware/firmware in China, to make it smaller and cheaper, and also easier to use.

2. Breath Analysis

Breath analysis has been mainly developed at ETH Zurich (Eidgenössische Technische Hochschule Zürich) and has now been declared a flagship project between ETH, University of Zurich and other institutions. Our company is closely collaborating with the headscientist to further develop the general technology and special applications.

The current equipment us quite large and expensive, as it includes a general-purpose mass spectrograph. There is already on the market an add-on to general-purpose mass spectrum analyzers which allows the detection of minute quantities of particular molecules; We have obtained the right to market this product in China and we intend to bring some demo units here whenever feasible.

In next phases, monitoring equipment for the exact dosage of chemotherapy medication, early lung cancer detection equipment, drug abuse detection equipment and several other special applications will be developed.
